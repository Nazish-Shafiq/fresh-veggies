Frequently Asked Questions

Which areas do you deliver to?
We provide fast and reliable delivery across all major areas of the city. You can check your area availability while placing your order.

Yes! All our vegetables are sourced fresh daily from the market and packed under hygienic conditions.
How long does delivery take?
We usually deliver within 24 hours after the order is placed. Same-day delivery is also available in selected areas.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Yes, you can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour after placing it.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash on Delivery, JazzCash, and Online Bank Transfers.
